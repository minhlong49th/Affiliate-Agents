# KNOWLEDGE: LP Framework Advertorial (FULL)

## 6. JOHN CRESTANI — 17-STEP FRAMEWORK
Full application: Advertorial LP only.
1. Hook
2. Identify the problem
3. Agitate the problem
4. Present the solution
5. Build credibility
6. List benefits
7. Social proof
8. Make the offer
9. Create urgency
10. State the price
11. Reduce risk
12. Restate benefits
13. Call to action
14. Warning / scarcity
15. P.S. line (Write this FIRST)
16. Close loop
17. Final CTA

### PAS 60% rule
Total word count × 0.6 = minimum words before brand/solution is introduced. No product mention before this threshold.

## 7. CRESTANI — THIRD-PERSON HERO DEVICE
Purpose: social proof through narrative.
Structure: [Hero name], [specific location], had [time period] trying to [action] without results... discovered [mechanism] through [specific source].
Rules:
- Hero must be a real person or fictional composite.
- Third-person narrative only.
